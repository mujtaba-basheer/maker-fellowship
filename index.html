Where makers are made

Every month, we offer $1000 to makers who are building useful side-projects to ship them within the next 4 weeks.

Register now

What do we take in return? Nothing. The thousand dollars come without any strings attached to them.

What good a maker is if they don’t ship. The purpose of the fellowship is to make more makers ship.

But after you ship your side-project, and it shows traction, and you want to evolve it into a startup, we’d want to invest up to $50k against the equity in the company. This should allow you to build an initial team, and focus on the product full-time for the next six to twelve months.

If the side project burns and fails, we all learn our lessons and move on.

What kinds of makers are eligible?

Everyone is welcome. But those with a useful idea/work-in-progress side project will be preferred.

Side projects come in all shapes and sizes. We qualify them based on their “usefulness.”

Usefulness comes first. Design comes second. Nothing else matters.
